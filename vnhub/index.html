Hub

Community platform combining Reddit + Quora style discussions

💰 Completely Free

What is Hub?

Hub is a community-driven discussion platform that combines the best features of Reddit and Quora. Share knowledge, ask questions, engage in meaningful discussions, and connect with like-minded individuals across various topics and interests.

Platform Features

💬

Discussion Forums

Organized forums for different topics with threaded discussions and voting.

❓

Q&A System

Ask questions and get expert answers from the community with upvoting system.

👥

User Profiles

Detailed user profiles with reputation scores, badges, and contribution history.

🏷️

Topic Tags

Organize content with tags and categories for easy discovery and navigation.

⭐

Voting System

Upvote and downvote posts and comments to highlight quality content.

🔍

Advanced Search

Powerful search functionality to find specific discussions, users, or topics.
