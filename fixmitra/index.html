What is FixMitra?

FixMitra is a comprehensive service platform designed to bridge the gap between users and verified on-site service providers. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, technician, or any other professional service, FixMitra makes it easy to find, book, and connect with trusted professionals in your area.

How FixMitra Works

1

Users Book Services

Customers browse available services and book appointments completely free of charge. No upfront costs or hidden fees for users.

2

Service Completion

Verified service providers complete the job to customer satisfaction, ensuring quality work and professional service delivery.

3

Commission Payment

Providers pay a small commission only after successful job completion, ensuring reliability and quality service for all parties.
